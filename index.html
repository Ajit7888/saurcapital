About Saur Capital

Founded in 2023, Saur Capital is a premier advisory firm specializing in infrastructure financing, project investments, and renewable energy sector consulting. We bring extensive experience and deep industry knowledge to help our clients navigate complex financial landscapes.

With a commitment to sustainable development and innovative financial solutions, we partner with businesses, investors, and project developers to unlock value and drive successful outcomes in the rapidly evolving energy and infrastructure sectors.

Our team of experts combines technical expertise with financial acumen to provide comprehensive advisory services tailored to each client's unique needs and objectives.

Founder & Managing Director

Kirandeep Singh

With over a decade of experience in infrastructure finance and renewable energy, Kirandeep founded Saur Capital to address the growing need for specialized financial advisory services in these sectors.

His extensive background includes successful roles in investment banking, project development, and financial consulting, with a particular focus on renewable energy projects across India and international markets.

Our Services

Investment Banking Solutions

We provide comprehensive capital raising services, including debt syndication, equity placement, and structured financing solutions tailored for infrastructure and renewable energy projects.

Our team assists with mergers & acquisitions, corporate finance advisory, and strategic partnerships to maximize value for our clients.

Project Development Solutions

We offer end-to-end project development advisory services, from feasibility studies and financial modeling to risk assessment and investment structuring for renewable energy initiatives.

Our expertise spans solar, wind, hydroelectric, and other clean energy technologies, ensuring optimal project design and financing structures.

Infrastructure Advisory Solutions

We deliver specialized consulting services for infrastructure projects, including public-private partnerships, concession agreements, and long-term operational structures.

Our team helps navigate regulatory frameworks, optimize capital deployment, and create sustainable financing plans for complex infrastructure initiatives.
