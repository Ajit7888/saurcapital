About Saur Capital

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by Kirandeep Singh, Saur Capital was established to address the growing need for specialized financial advisory services in the infrastructure and renewable energy sectors. Our name "Saur" derives from the Sanskrit word for "solar," reflecting our commitment to sustainable energy solutions.

With deep industry knowledge and extensive experience across infrastructure financing, renewable energy project development, and strategic consulting, Saur Capital has quickly emerged as a trusted partner for businesses, investors, and project developers seeking to navigate the complex landscapes of energy transition and infrastructure development.

Based in Gurugram, we serve clients throughout India and international markets, providing tailored advisory solutions that combine financial expertise with technical knowledge to drive successful outcomes in an ever-evolving sector.
