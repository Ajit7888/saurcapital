Our Services

Comprehensive financial advisory solutions for infrastructure and renewable energy

Investment Banking Solutions

Comprehensive Capital Solutions

Our Investment Banking Solutions provide clients with strategic financial advisory services to optimize capital structure and secure funding for infrastructure and renewable energy projects.

With deep industry knowledge and extensive market connections, we help clients navigate complex financial landscapes and achieve their capital-raising objectives.

  • Debt Syndication: Structuring and arranging debt packages from diverse sources including commercial banks, development financial institutions, and specialized lenders.
  • Equity Placement: Identifying and securing equity investments from strategic partners, private equity firms, and institutional investors.
  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Providing comprehensive M&A advisory, from target identification to transaction execution.
  • Financial Restructuring: Developing and implementing financial restructuring strategies to optimize capital structures and improve financial performance.
  • Capital Markets Access: Supporting clients in accessing public markets through IPOs, bond issuances, and other capital market instruments.

Project Development Solutions

End-to-End Project Advisory

Our Project Development Solutions provide comprehensive advisory services throughout the project lifecycle, from initial concept to financial close and beyond.

We work closely with developers, investors, and stakeholders to optimize project structures, mitigate risks, and maximize returns on renewable energy and infrastructure initiatives.

  • Feasibility Studies: Conducting thorough market, technical, and financial assessments to evaluate project viability.
  • Financial Modeling: Developing comprehensive financial models to forecast project performance, cash flows, and returns.
  • Risk Assessment: Identifying, analyzing, and developing mitigation strategies for project risks.
  • Project Structuring: Designing optimal legal, financial, and operational structures to enhance project value.
  • Technology Assessment: Evaluating renewable energy technologies and solutions for specific project applications.

Infrastructure Advisory Solutions

Specialized Infrastructure Consulting

Our Infrastructure Advisory Solutions provide specialized consulting services to navigate the complex regulatory, financial, and operational challenges of infrastructure development.

With expertise across various infrastructure sectors, we help clients optimize capital deployment, manage risks, and create sustainable long-term structures for successful project implementation.

  • Public-Private Partnerships: Structuring and advisory for PPP projects, including concession agreements and risk allocation.
  • Regulatory Navigation: Guiding clients through complex regulatory frameworks and approval processes.
  • Contract Advisory: Assisting with contract negotiation, drafting, and review for infrastructure projects.
  • Procurement Strategy: Developing effective procurement strategies for infrastructure components and services.
  • Policy Advisory: Providing insights on infrastructure policy developments and their implications for investments.
